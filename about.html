About Tayyib Crops

Pure, local, and grown with love

🌿 Our Mission

At Tayyib Crops, we grow what we feed our own kids — pure, chemical-free oyster mushrooms and seasonal produce. True to the name Tayyib, meaning “pure,” everything we offer is grown with care and shared with delight, from our family to yours.

🔭 Our Vision

Inspired by the meaning of Tayyib — “pure” — we aim to stay small and sincere, building a local circle that values clean, homegrown food. At Tayyib Crops, we grow with love and share with intention.

💚 Our Core Values

  • 🌱 Purity: We grow food that lives up to our name — Tayyib, meaning pure. No chemicals. No shortcuts. Just clean, honest produce we trust for our own kids.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family First: Our farm is run by family, for families. Every mushroom and fruit is grown with the same care we give to feeding our loved ones.
  • 🏡 Local & Personal: We don’t aim to go big. We stay close, sharing our produce only with nearby homes — keeping things fresh, direct, and personal.
  • 🧺 Careful Growing: We believe slow, thoughtful growing beats fast farming. We hand-harvest and handle everything with care.
  • 🤝 Integrity Over Scale: We’re not a factory. We’re a family sharing what we grow. Every sale carries our name, so we keep it honest, always.
